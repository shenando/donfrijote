Frequently Asked Questions
  • photo_libraryHow will I get my design?
    For digital art, I will send you a high quality digital file. For traditional art, I can arrange shipping and add that to the commission price.
  • format_color_fillWhat can you make?
    Anything you want! You can see my samples for ideas of whay I can do but I love trying new styles and subjects. If I can't successfully create what you want, I'll be honest and tel you before accepting any payment.
  • loyaltyCan prices change?
    These prices are the base estimate for a single figure with a simple background/no background. Things like more figures/characters, a complete background, or a realistic/traditional painting art style would all cost more but I would discuss and finalize that before any payments are made. I will not, however, change the price of a commission if that change wasn't agreed upon before.
